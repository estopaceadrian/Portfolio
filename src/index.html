About me

I have vast experience providing customer service via email and live chat. In addition to my current position, I work on projects involving social media marketing, email marketing, and lead generation. I committed to studying web programming every evening after work beginning in early 2021, with an emphasis on React, PHP Laravel, MySQL, and Bootstrap CSS. In May 2022, I joined in Bootcamp to further my understanding of web development. As a final capstone, I created the full-stack e-commerce website Ronin Scooter PH, which helped me graduate as one of the Bootcamp's top students.

I studied a variety of technologies after graduating from Bootcamp and started working on personal projects using Ruby on Rails, NodeJS, Express JS, NextJS, PostgreSQL, Mongo DB, and React Native. I also joined the #100DaysOfCode twitter community to stay inspired and track my development.
